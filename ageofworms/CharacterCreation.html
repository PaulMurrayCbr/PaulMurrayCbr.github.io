Races

Races may be anything up to LA +2 as usual. Note that we will be keeping track of XP, and ECL penaties will apply. Remember that we all spent money on Eberron books, so Eberron races are ok. Note that elves are rare in Eberron, and will therefore attract a +1 LA.


Kindly note, Steve, that monstrous characters have racial hit die as well as level adjustment. A minotar character starts at ECL 9: 6 HD of Minotaur, +2 LA, and one character level. Probably a mit much to be sending through a level 1 dungeon. Likewise, an ogre starts with 4 levels of giant, +2 LA, and on class level for a total ECL of 7.


If you like, we can use the Savage Species rules. In this variant, you take “levels” in the monster class and the benefits come on-line gradually.


For an ogre, we would go something like:

1 – +1d8 HD, size M, reach 5', +2 Str, +1 Con, -4 Int, -2 Cha, darkvision

2 – +1d8 HD, +1 Str, -1 Dex

3 – LA +1, Size L, reach 10', +2 Str, +1 Con, -1 Cha

4 – +1d8 HD, +2 Str, +1 Con

5 – +1d8 HD, +1 Str, -1 Dex, -1 Cha

6 – LA +1, +2 Str, +2 Str, +1 Con,


And your first character level would be Barbarian 1 when everyone else is level 7. In other words – WOTC rig things so that players won't want to play monsters as characters.
