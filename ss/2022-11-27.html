Our heroes, after healing up, assay the canibal camp.

But first, a spot of bother in the morning. They are assailed by a botfly swarm. They have little way to deal with it, and Volka has to drop the daily: Call Lightning. It is doubly effective against a swarm, and disperses it in one hit. But, well, that's the daily gone.

On to the real encounter!

The village is almost empty. There is a building with 6 skeletons, but they do not attack. The party place a barrel of pitch on the roof and ignite it, the flames destroying four of the skeltons. But, it seems they have their orders. They still do not attack.

Presently, a pair of cannibals emerge onto the fortified balcony of the lighthouse and hurl javelins, and it's on.

Snid climbs. Nix jumps. And Kraig goes through the door. But as the battle inside the lighthouse heats up, the witch Malikadna emerges through the building where the skeletons are, bringing with four skeletons.

Two guarding her more closely, because like if everyone was a cannibal and you had skeletons you'd be leaving them all off in a hut somewhere.

Eventually, they all wind up in a narrow corridor at the base of the lighthouse. Malakidna takes cover behind a defending skeleton and hurls curses. They work … but without someone actually doing damage, debuffing the enemy does little. All of her tribe and all but one of her skeletons are fallen. She summons a Lemure, which the party have not dealt with before. It's immunity to cold stymies Volka's magic attacks.

But all in all - too little, too late. The fight is long and awkward, but she falls. Our heroes are victorious yet again.
