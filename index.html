Battlemat Grids:

a1Grid.svg a1Grid.pdf a2Grid.svg a2Grid.pdf a3Grid.svg a3Grid.pdf a4Grid.svg a4Grid.pdf

I get these printed at OfficeWorks and ask for the "architectural drawing" rate, which is way, way cheaper than full colour. Make sure you get them printed at 100% scale - the sizes are correct. Then I colour the paper with cheap watercolour or instant coffee, and back to officeworks to get laminated. Whiteboard marker wipes off, sometimes you need a little isopropyl. About $20 for a a1-sized battlemat that you can use for years.
